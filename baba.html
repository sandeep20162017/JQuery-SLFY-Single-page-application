ABOUT Aptitude Baba

Aptitude BABA is an Educational Multimedia Company based in Amritsar that specializes in APTITUDE Preparation. It is focused on providing an enhanced educational experience to students by leveraging technology. At Aptitude BABA we enable individuals to realize their potential and achieve their dreams. This is our core ideology and is firmly grounded on our focus on academic excellence, technological innovation, and deep rooted domain expertise. Aptitude BABA is the Brain Child of Rishabh Arora. Aptitude BABA is his second venture, before this he started with GREKEY in the year 2015. GREKEY is a GRE Preparation institute which in a period of about two years established itself as the leading Center for GRE Preparation in Amritsar. For Year 2016 GREKEY accounts for about 75% of all 310+ GRE Results in Amritsar. We here at Aptitude BABA plan to emulate such success in all Aptitude based exams, with our prime focus being on Campus Placements, Banking Jobs, SSC and other Government Jobs.
What makes us Different...?
1) The most important difference is the philosophy of our Work:
“A student never fails, a teacher does”

Our Work ethic is remarkably different from any other Aptitude Preparation institute; we believe that if a student does not improve on scores then it is our moral failure than the failure of the student. While others grade themselves by the success of their best students, our performance parameter is the improvement of our weakest students.

2) Special Focus on English:

While others leave Vocabulary preparation to God’s Mercy we here at Aptitude Baba have created VISUAL DICTIONARY. It is a collection of 1500+ Words which are repeated in SSC and Bank PO exams. This tool actually comes close to being a God, as the recall value of this method is almost 95%.

4) Most comprehensive Program:

Our curriculum covers 8,000+ questions of Maths, Reasoning and English. This level of detail ensures that the student is able to absolutely master every concept and is ready to crack every god damn aptitude exam. (With an exception of CAT and XAT)

While most others offer a curriculum of one Lecture every day lasting for about 12 weeks, our flagship program called "LEVIATHAN" provides two lectures of 1.5 Hours each everyday for about 12 weeks. To know more about our Various Programs head on the PROGRAMS.

5) Active Use of Video Library*:

We at Aptitude baba are also developing a massive Video library which would help augment doubt solving sessions for students. Just imagine holding your phone towards the worksheet and a video popping out that explains the same question you have been struck at.

* Video Library is under development and will be available to students on ad hoc basis to complement lectures delivered in class. It is a revision /doubt clearing tool only and by no means intends to replace classroom lectures.

Program LEVIATHAN

LEVIATHAN

The Flagship program of Aptitude Baba,is an extended course designed for evolving needs of students preparing for government jobs. The program offers more than 160 lecture of 1.5 Hours each, along with 30 Hours of Doubt taking and discussion; it is by far the most comprehensive program. The program delivers 10 to 12 Lectures every week spread over a period of about 3 to 4 months.

Program Break Up.

  1. 30 Lectures of Visual Vocabulary covering 1500 frequently used words.
  2. 10 Lectures of Reading Comprehensions.
  3. 18 Lectures of English Usage.
  4. 10 Lectures of Verbal Logic.
  5. 50 Lectures of Maths covering 3000+ Questions.
  6. 20 Lectures of Analytical Reasoning.
  7. 20 Lectures of General Knowledge and General Awareness Covering 1000 frequently asked questions .
  8. 10 Specialized workshops for Accelerated preparation.
  9. Visual Dictionary – A set of three DVD’s to revise all Words discussed in class.
  10. Book Set – A set of 12 Books Published by Aptitude Baba that cover 8000+ Questions of Maths, Reasoning and English.

Validity : 1 Year

Program HELLFIRE

HELLFIRE

A fast track program designed to help engineering students prepare for the first round of the campus recruitments. The program not only provides basic Aptitude Training but also provides specific books and tests for TCS and Nagarro that have been created after deep analysis of previous year campus recruitment papers. The Program offers 6 to 8 Lectures every week spread over a period of about 2 to 4 months.

Program Break Up.

  1. 35 Lectures of Mathematics covering 2000 + Questions.
  2. 28 Lectures of English Usage.
  3. 20 Lectures of Analytical Reasoning.
  4. 20 Lectures of GD, Interviews, Extempore and Resume making.
  5. 10 Mock Interviews (Recorded for Student feedback).
  6. 5 Day Workshop for TCS.
  7. Book Set – A set of 8 Books Published by Aptitude Baba that cover 5000+ Questions of Maths, Reasoning and English..

Validity : 5 Months
